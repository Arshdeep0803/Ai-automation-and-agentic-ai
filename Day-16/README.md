# Day 16 – Customer Support AI Bot

Today, I built a **Customer Support AI Bot** using an AI automation workflow. The goal of this bot is to answer customer questions automatically, reduce manual work, and provide quick and accurate responses.

## What I Learned

* Designed an AI-powered customer support workflow.
* Used an AI Agent to understand user queries and generate relevant responses.
* Connected the bot with a **Knowledge Base** so it could answer questions using stored information instead of giving random replies.
* Learned how AI agents process user input, retrieve information, and generate natural-language responses.
* Improved the workflow by testing different customer queries and checking the bot's responses.

## Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base is a collection of information that the AI bot uses to answer customer questions. It can include:

* Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
* Company policies
* Product information
* Service details
* Troubleshooting guides

When a customer asks a question, the AI Agent searches the Knowledge Base, finds the most relevant information, and returns an appropriate answer.

## AI Agent

The AI Agent acts as the brain of the customer support bot. Its responsibilities include:

* Understanding customer messages.
* Searching the Knowledge Base for relevant information.
* Generating clear and helpful responses.
* Handling multiple customer queries efficiently.
* Providing a better customer support experience with minimal human intervention.

## Benefits of the Customer Support Bot

* Provides instant responses to customers.
* Available 24/7.
* Reduces the workload of support teams.
* Delivers consistent and accurate answers.
* Improves customer satisfaction and response time.

## Conclusion

This project helped me understand how AI Agents and Knowledge Bases work together to automate customer support. I learned how intelligent automation can improve customer service by providing fast, reliable, and context-aware responses.
